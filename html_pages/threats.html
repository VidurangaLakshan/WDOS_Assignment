Threats to Turtles

Turtles face a variety of threats, both natural and human-made.

Threat 1 : Pollution

Pollution, such as plastic debris and oil spills, can harm and kill turtles directly or indirectly by contaminating their food sources.

Threat 2 : Invasive species

Invasive species, such as feral pigs and rats, can predate on turtle nests and hatchlings, reducing the survival rates of turtle populations.

Threat 3 : Coastal development

Development of coastal areas for tourism or infrastructure can lead to loss of nesting beaches and feeding grounds for turtles.
